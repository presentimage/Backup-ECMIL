<p style="text-align: center;" data-css="tve-u-1673d2f9283">The Steel of The Season</p>
<p data-css="tve-u-1673d2f9295">We find ourselves at the end of another exciting year, gladly spared from the ravages of major wind driven storms, still very much aware of the considerable rain and flooding within our region.  To this end, we place considerable focus on water management and materials, through our distribution of product information and support.</p>
<p data-css="tve-u-1673d2f9296">ECMIL continues and intensifies its efforts to assist recovery, in some areas and supply new and ongoing projects in others. Our exceptional <span style="color: #0063be;"><a href="http://ecmil.net/an-ecmil-roof/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Colorbond</a></span>® and <span style="color: #0063be;"><a href="http://ecmil.net/an-ecmil-roof/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Zincalume</a></span>® quality, breadth of roofing styles in <span style="color: #0063be;"><a href="http://ecmil.net/roofing-viewer/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">both colour and profile</a></span>, <span style="color: #0063be;"><a href="http://ecmil.net/roof-accessories/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">comprehensive range of supporting accessories</a></span>, <span style="color: #0063be;"><a href="http://ecmil.net/steel-fabrication/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">reliable steel fabrication</a></span>, <span style="color: #0063be;"><a href="http://ecmil.net/pvc-pipes-conduits-by-ecpil/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">PVC pipes and conduit</a></span>, <span style="color: #0063be;"><a href="http://ecmil.net/ecmil-news-articles-2/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">product advice</a> </span>and excellent ex-factory pricing have placed consumer focus on our business approach.</p>
<p data-css="tve-u-1673d2f9297">We are please to say that ECMIL products and services continue to be well received throughout the region.  Presently, pre Christmas interest suggests an upsurge of positive construction throughout the islands.  May we say <span style="color: #0063be;">ECMIL The Steel of The Season?</span></p>
<span class="tve_image_frame" style="width: 100%;"><a href="http://ecmil.net/master/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/cuba.001.jpeg" rel=""><img class="tve_image wp-image-6892" style="width: 100%;" title="cuba.001" src="//ecmil.net/master/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/cuba.001.jpeg" alt="" width="1024" height="768" data-id="6892" /></a></span>
<p data-css="tve-u-1673d2f9298">We travelled to Cuba, to attend the <span style="color: #0063be;" data-css="tve-u-1673d2f929a">Havana International Fair,</span><strong><span style="color: #0063be;" data-css="tve-u-1673d2f929a"> FIHAV 2018</span></strong>, for a third consecutive year.</p>
<p data-css="tve-u-1673d2f9298">This brought considerable interest from Spanish speaking territories within and adjacent to our region.</p>
<p data-css="tve-u-1673d2f9299">ECMIL continues to explore these fresh opportunities, while improving our quality of product and service, always customised to meet the precise needs of the Caribbean.</p>
